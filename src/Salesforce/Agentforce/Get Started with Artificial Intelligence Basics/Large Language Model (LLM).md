Are advanced computer models designed to understand and generate human-like text. They're trained on vast amounts of text data to learn patterns, language structures, and relationships between words and sentences.

Predict one [[Token]] (for example, a word or character) at a time, building a sequence from start to finish. Given a request, they try to predict the next token, and the next, and the next, and so on.

Predictions made by LLMs refer to their ability to generate or complete text based on patterns they've seen during training.
