You can truncate a custom object, except when your org has reached its limit on allowed custom objects.
You cannot truncate standard objects or custom objects referenced by another object through a lookup field, or on the master side of a master-detail relationship, referenced in a reporting snapshot, have a custom index or external ID, or activated skinny tables.

Erases all records currrently sitting in the custom objects's Recycle Bin, also as custom object's history. related events, tasks, notes, and attachments.

1. Go to Setup
2. Search for User Interface in Quick Find
3. In Setup section, mark Enable Custom Object Truncate.
4. Save.
This permanently removes all of its records. Its still in the Deleted Objects list for 15 days, and continue to count toward tour organization's limits.
