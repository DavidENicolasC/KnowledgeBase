It´s a [[Special Section Styles]] that precedes the first chapter of a book or a book part.

Can only be used when the document type is [[book Document Type]].

can contain [[Preface Subsection]]s.

When a book contains parts, can be defined as either a level 0 section `=` or level 1 section `==`.

When a book doesn’t contain parts, must be defined as a level 1 section (`==`).

```
= Book Title
:doctype: book

[preface]
== Our Preface

I awoke one morning and was confronted by the dark and stormy eyes of the chinchilla. She had conquered the mountain of government reports that had eroded into several minor foothills and a creeping alluvial plain of loose papers.
```
