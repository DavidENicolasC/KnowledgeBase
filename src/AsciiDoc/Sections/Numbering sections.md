= Title

Level 1 `==` through level 3 `====` section titles are prefixed with arabic num­bers in the form of 1., 1.1., etc.

Can be set and unset via the document header, CLI, and API.

You can reduce or increase the section levels that get numbered in the whole document with the [[sectnumlevels Attribute]].

You can control whether a section is numbered on a section by [[Section Basis]].
