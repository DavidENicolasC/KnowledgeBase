There are two types:
- [[Flow Index Term]]
- [[Concealed Index Term]]
```
The Lady of the Lake, her arm clad in the purest shimmering samite,
held aloft Excalibur from the bosom of the water,
signifying by divine providence that I, ((Arthur)),
was to carry Excalibur(((Sword, Broadsword, Excalibur))).
That is why I am your king. Shut up! Will you shut up?!
Burn her anyway! I'm not a witch.
Look, my liege! We found them.

indexterm2:[Lancelot] was one of the Knights of the Round Table.
indexterm:[knight, Knight of the Round Table, Lancelot]
```

Double parenthesis form adds a primary index term and includes the term in the generated output.

Triple parenthesis form allows for an optional second and third index term and _does not_ include the terms in the generated output.

If one of the terms contains a comma, you must surround that segment in double quotes.
```
I, King Arthur.
indexterm:[knight, "Arthur, King"]

I, King Arthur.
(((knight, "Arthur, King")))
```
